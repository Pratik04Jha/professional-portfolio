## About this project

Graviton redefines how you interact with AI. A unified platform that combines conversational intelligence, media generation, and community-driven personas into one seamless experience.

### Three Modes, Infinite Possibilities

| Mode           | Description |
|----------------|-------------|
| **Agentic Mode** | Tackles complex challenges—from advanced coding problems to strategic planning. Breaks down intricate tasks and delivers production-ready solutions with step-by-step reasoning. |
| **Adaptive Mode** | Learns from your conversation patterns, delivering instant, contextually relevant answers that evolve with each interaction. Built for speed without sacrificing intelligence. |
| **Chat Mode**     | Handles everyday queries with natural, conversational intelligence—your go-to companion for quick insights, brainstorming, and daily assistance. |

### Multimodal Intelligence

Upload and analyze **images, videos, and PDFs**. Graviton doesn't just read your files—it understands them, extracting insights, answering questions, and making connections across any format. Perfect for research, content analysis, and document processing.

### Professional Media Generation

Transform ideas into reality with our comprehensive media suite:

| Media Type     | Capabilities |
|----------------|--------------|
| **Images**       | Generate stunning visuals from text descriptions |
| **Videos**       | Create professional video content with AI assistance |
| **Presentations**| Build polished slide decks in minutes |
| **Spreadsheets** | Generate data tables and analysis documents |
| **Documents**    | Create reports, diagrams, and custom file formats |

### Persona Ecosystem

Dive into a vibrant community of AI personalities. Chat with pre-built personas ranging from **creative writers to technical experts, business advisors to language tutors**. Or architect your own custom AI character with unique traits, knowledge domains, and conversation styles.

Share your creations with the community, discover what others have built, and find the perfect AI companion for any task or conversation.

### Intelligent Organization

| Feature         | Description |
|-----------------|-------------|
| **Smart History** | Every conversation is automatically saved and searchable. Create folders, rename chats, tag important discussions, and never lose context. Advanced search lets you find any conversation in seconds. |
| **Media Library** | Automatically organizes every image, video, and file—whether generated by AI or uploaded by you. Smart categorization, instant retrieval, and powerful filters make managing your media effortless. |
